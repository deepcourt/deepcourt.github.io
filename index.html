What I've built (hardware)

            

                

                    
                    

                        
26 Above pedal straps

                        
Pedal straps made out of decommissioned Italian car seat belts. Initially bought a pair from a shop for my custom fixed gear bicycle. They were made out of leather and metal. After a few rides, I realised the metal attachment was damaging my shoes, so I built my own without metal parts and with a more durable material. Shared on fixedforum.it, the largest fixed gear bicycle forum in Italy, and ended up selling a few.

                        

                            Seat belt
                            Velcro
                            Reinforced seam
                            Singer sewing machine
                        
                    

                

                

                    
                    

                        
"H." custom fixed gear bicycle

                        
Sourced the Italian steel frame from subito.it, stripped the vintage Bianchi brakes and sold them to recover 30% of the initial investment, sourced the wheels directly from a factory in Bologna, replaced drivetrain, steering and brakes components. Painted in "bianco elettrodomestico" and "nero lucido". Sold at ~90% gross profit margin.

                        

                            Industrial-grade sandblasting
                            Columbus steel frame
                            Industrial-grade powder coating paint
