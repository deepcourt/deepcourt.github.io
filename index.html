How I Can Help

My consulting work is focused on creating tangible financial and operational results. I offer services in three core areas:

(1) Food Delivery & Logistics Optimisation

The delivery market is defined by razor-thin margins. I help multi-location restaurants, QSRs, and last-mile logistics firms protect their profit and expand effectively.

What I do: I perform a deep analysis of your delivery channel performance and unit economics. I also review and help renegotiate your commercial partnership terms with delivery apps and suppliers to improve your margins.

The result: A clear, data-driven plan to increase profitability per order and secure more favourable commercial agreements.

(2) AI & Process Automation for SMEs

For businesses generating £2M-£20M ($2.7M-$27M) a year, manual processes are a silent growth killer. Much like when I built an automated CRM workflow at Deliveroo that acquired 120+ leads a day with zero marketing spend.

What I do: I analyse your current finance and operational workflows, identify the most costly manual tasks, and build lightweight automation tools to handle them.

The result: Thousands of pounds saved in labour costs and hundreds of hours returned to your team to focus on high-value work.

(3) Growth & Operations for Series A+ Startups

Post-Series A, the challenge is building a scalable organisation. Having managed deals and negotiated term sheets from the VC side, I help leadership navigate this phase with an investor's perspective.

What I do: I work with your team on market expansion strategy, financial modelling, building performance dashboards, and structuring commercial partnerships.

The result: A more resilient, efficient organisation that grows faster and negotiates from a position of strength with partners and investors.
